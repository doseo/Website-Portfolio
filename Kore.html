Act

There is a lot of people who have brilliant ideas in the world. However, most people only dream about their bright ideas while entrepreneurs put them into actions. For the golden but vanished ideas that people only conceive, we, KORE, cluster a group of intelligent entrepreneurs to make those flashing ideas to be alive in the real world.

Impact

As an entrepreneurial group, KORE aims to make a strong impact on the people, community and the world. As a group of young intellectuals who study in various fields and majors, KORE intends to make a positive impact on every single member in strengthening their fields of studies and networking.

Network

After all, KORE is a group that is founded and operated by young and enlightened university students. The greatest achievements of all time generally come from the works between the best friends. Thus, we, KORE, shoot to create a caring environment so the members not only can express themselves in an open setting but also can feel their ‘home’ while working for KORE.

Dare To Try!


Many people resist being changed, as their anxiety often outweighs the positive outcome that the change may bring about. Thus, unfortunately, most of the university students who are relatively young and new to society, also worry about the change before facing it. However, we, KORE, motivate our members to get out of their comfort zones but ‘dare to try’ anything they want so that they can impact the world.
